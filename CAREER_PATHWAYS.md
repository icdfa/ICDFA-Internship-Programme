# ICDFA Internship Programme -- Career Pathways & Industry Opportunities

**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** April 2026

---

## Overview

This document outlines career pathways for ICDFA Cybersecurity Internship Programme (CIP) graduates. Graduates are prepared for entry-level to mid-level positions in cybersecurity, with clear advancement pathways to senior and leadership roles.

---

## Track A: Ethical Hacking Career Pathways

### Entry-Level Positions (0-2 Years)

Upon completing Track A, graduates are qualified for:

**1. Penetration Tester (Junior)**
- Conduct authorized security assessments and penetration tests
- Identify vulnerabilities and security weaknesses
- Document findings in professional reports
- **Salary Range:** $60,000 - $85,000
- **Required Skills:** Metasploit, Nmap, Burp Suite, networking fundamentals
- **Certifications:** CEH, OSCP (optional)

**2. Security Operations Center (SOC) Analyst**
- Monitor security alerts and events
- Investigate potential security incidents
- Respond to security threats
- **Salary Range:** $55,000 - $75,000
- **Required Skills:** Log analysis, SIEM tools, incident response, networking
- **Certifications:** Security+, GCIH (optional)

**3. Network Security Engineer**
- Design and implement network security controls
- Configure firewalls, VPNs, and access controls
- Monitor network traffic for threats
- **Salary Range:** $65,000 - $90,000
- **Required Skills:** Firewalls, VPNs, network protocols, Palo Alto Networks
- **Certifications:** Network+, CCNA Security (optional)

**4. Security Analyst**
- Analyze security vulnerabilities and threats
- Recommend security improvements
- Assist with security assessments
- **Salary Range:** $55,000 - $80,000
- **Required Skills:** Vulnerability assessment, threat analysis, reporting
- **Certifications:** Security+, CEH (optional)

### Mid-Level Positions (2-5 Years)

With experience and additional certifications, graduates can advance to:

**1. Penetration Tester (Senior)**
- Lead complex penetration testing engagements
- Develop custom exploitation techniques
- Mentor junior penetration testers
- **Salary Range:** $90,000 - $130,000
- **Required Skills:** Advanced exploitation, custom tool development, leadership
- **Certifications:** OSCP, GWAPT, GPEN

**2. Incident Response Specialist**
- Lead incident response and forensic investigations
- Develop incident response procedures
- Conduct post-incident analysis and reporting
- **Salary Range:** $85,000 - $120,000
- **Required Skills:** Forensics, incident response, threat analysis, leadership
- **Certifications:** GCIH, GCFE, GCIA

**3. Security Architect**
- Design comprehensive security solutions
- Evaluate and select security technologies
- Develop security policies and standards
- **Salary Range:** $100,000 - $150,000
- **Required Skills:** Security design, risk management, strategic thinking
- **Certifications:** CISSP, CCSK, GSLC

**4. Threat Intelligence Analyst**
- Collect and analyze threat intelligence
- Identify emerging threats and attack patterns
- Develop threat intelligence reports
- **Salary Range:** $80,000 - $120,000
- **Required Skills:** Threat analysis, intelligence gathering, reporting
- **Certifications:** GIAC Certified Intrusion Analyst (GCIA)

### Senior & Leadership Positions (5+ Years)

With extensive experience, graduates can pursue:

**1. Chief Information Security Officer (CISO)**
- Oversee all security operations and strategy
- Manage security budgets and teams
- Report to executive leadership
- **Salary Range:** $150,000 - $300,000+
- **Required Skills:** Strategic leadership, risk management, business acumen
- **Certifications:** CISSP, CCSK, MBA (optional)

**2. Security Director**
- Lead security teams and initiatives
- Develop security strategies and policies
- Manage security budgets
- **Salary Range:** $120,000 - $200,000
- **Required Skills:** Team leadership, strategic planning, business management
- **Certifications:** CISSP, GSLC

**3. Consulting Manager**
- Lead security consulting engagements
- Manage consulting teams
- Develop consulting methodologies
- **Salary Range:** $130,000 - $220,000
- **Required Skills:** Consulting expertise, client management, leadership
- **Certifications:** CISSP, GPEN, GWAPT

**4. Security Researcher**
- Conduct advanced security research
- Publish research findings and vulnerabilities
- Develop new security techniques
- **Salary Range:** $100,000 - $180,000
- **Required Skills:** Research methodology, technical expertise, publication
- **Certifications:** PhD (optional), advanced certifications

## Track B: Digital Forensics Career Pathways

### Entry-Level Positions (0-2 Years)

Upon completing Track B, graduates are qualified for:

**1. Digital Forensics Examiner (Junior)**
- Conduct digital forensic investigations
- Acquire and preserve digital evidence
- Analyze forensic artifacts
- **Salary Range:** $55,000 - $80,000
- **Required Skills:** Autopsy, EnCase, file system analysis, chain of custody
- **Certifications:** GCFE, ACE (optional)

**2. Incident Response Analyst**
- Respond to security incidents
- Collect and preserve evidence
- Conduct preliminary investigation
- **Salary Range:** $60,000 - $85,000
- **Required Skills:** Incident response, evidence handling, analysis
- **Certifications:** GCIH, ECIH (optional)

**3. Forensic Analyst**
- Analyze digital evidence
- Identify indicators of compromise
- Prepare forensic reports
- **Salary Range:** $55,000 - $80,000
- **Required Skills:** Evidence analysis, forensic tools, reporting
- **Certifications:** GCFE, GCIA (optional)

**4. Computer Security Incident Response Team (CSIRT) Member**
- Respond to security incidents
- Coordinate incident response efforts
- Provide technical support during incidents
- **Salary Range:** $60,000 - $85,000
- **Required Skills:** Incident response, technical troubleshooting, communication
- **Certifications:** GCIH, ECIH (optional)

### Mid-Level Positions (2-5 Years)

With experience and additional certifications, graduates can advance to:

**1. Senior Digital Forensics Examiner**
- Lead complex forensic investigations
- Develop forensic methodologies
- Mentor junior examiners
- **Salary Range:** $85,000 - $125,000
- **Required Skills:** Advanced forensics, custom tool development, leadership
- **Certifications:** GCFE, GCIA, GPEN

**2. Incident Response Manager**
- Lead incident response teams
- Develop incident response procedures
- Coordinate with external agencies
- **Salary Range:** $90,000 - $135,000
- **Required Skills:** Team leadership, incident management, strategic planning
- **Certifications:** GCIH, GCIA, CISSP

**3. Forensic Consultant**
- Provide forensic consulting services
- Conduct independent investigations
- Provide expert witness testimony
- **Salary Range:** $100,000 - $150,000
- **Required Skills:** Forensic expertise, consulting, expert testimony
- **Certifications:** GCFE, GCIA, GPEN

**4. Threat Intelligence Analyst**
- Analyze forensic findings for threat intelligence
- Identify attack patterns and adversaries
- Develop threat intelligence reports
- **Salary Range:** $80,000 - $120,000
- **Required Skills:** Threat analysis, forensic analysis, intelligence gathering
- **Certifications:** GCIA, GCFE

### Senior & Leadership Positions (5+ Years)

With extensive experience, graduates can pursue:

**1. Chief Information Security Officer (CISO)**
- Oversee all security operations and strategy
- Manage security budgets and teams
- Report to executive leadership
- **Salary Range:** $150,000 - $300,000+
- **Required Skills:** Strategic leadership, risk management, business acumen
- **Certifications:** CISSP, CCSK, MBA (optional)

**2. Forensic Director**
- Lead forensic teams and initiatives
- Develop forensic strategies and procedures
- Manage forensic budgets
- **Salary Range:** $120,000 - $200,000
- **Required Skills:** Team leadership, strategic planning, business management
- **Certifications:** CISSP, GCFE, GCIA

**3. Consulting Partner**

- Develop consulting strategies

- **Salary Range:** $150,000 - $300,000+

## Industry Sectors & Opportunities

### Cybersecurity Consulting Firms

Companies like Deloitte, Accenture, PwC, and smaller boutique firms offer:

- Penetration testing and security assessments
- Incident response and forensics
- Security architecture and design
- Compliance and risk management

**Career Path:** Analyst → Senior Analyst → Manager → Partner

**Salary Range:** $55,000 - $300,000+ depending on level

### Financial Services & Banking

Banks, insurance companies, and fintech firms offer:

- Security operations and incident response
- Fraud investigation and forensics
- Compliance and risk management
- Threat intelligence and analysis

**Career Path:** Analyst → Senior Analyst → Manager → Director

**Salary Range:** $60,000 - $250,000+ depending on level

### Government & Defense

Federal agencies, military, and defense contractors offer:

- Cybersecurity operations
- Incident response and forensics
- Threat intelligence
- Security clearance opportunities

**Career Path:** Analyst → Senior Analyst → Manager → Director

**Salary Range:** $55,000 - $200,000+ depending on level and clearance

### Technology Companies

Tech giants (Google, Microsoft, Amazon) and startups offer:

- Security operations and incident response
- Vulnerability research and exploitation
- Cloud security
- Product security

**Career Path:** Security Engineer → Senior Engineer → Manager → Director

**Salary Range:** $80,000 - $300,000+ depending on level and company

### Energy & Critical Infrastructure

Utilities, power companies, and industrial firms offer:

- ICS/SCADA security
- Critical infrastructure protection
- Incident response
- Compliance and risk management

**Career Path:** Analyst → Senior Analyst → Manager → Director

**Salary Range:** $65,000 - $220,000+ depending on level

### Healthcare

Hospitals, insurance companies, and healthcare IT firms offer:

- HIPAA compliance and security
- Incident response and forensics
- Threat intelligence
- Security operations

**Career Path:** Analyst → Senior Analyst → Manager → Director

**Salary Range:** $55,000 - $200,000+ depending on level

## Certification Roadmap

### Year 1: Foundation Certifications

- CompTIA Security+ (or equivalent)
- Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH) - Track A
- GIAC Certified Incident Handler (GCIH) - Track B

### Year 2-3: Intermediate Certifications

- GIAC Certified Intrusion Analyst (GCIA)
- Offensive Security Certified Professional (OSCP) - Track A
- GIAC Certified Forensic Examiner (GCFE) - Track B

### Year 4+: Advanced Certifications

- Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP)
- GIAC Security Leadership Certification (GSLC)
- GIAC Penetration Tester (GPEN) - Track A
- GIAC Web Application Penetration Tester (GWAPT) - Track A

## Salary Expectations

### Entry-Level (0-2 Years)

- **Average Salary:** $60,000 - $80,000
- **Range:** $50,000 - $100,000 depending on location and employer

### Mid-Level (2-5 Years)

- **Average Salary:** $90,000 - $120,000
- **Range:** $75,000 - $150,000 depending on location and employer

### Senior-Level (5+ Years)

- **Average Salary:** $130,000 - $180,000
- **Range:** $100,000 - $250,000+ depending on location and employer

### Executive-Level (CISO, Director)

- **Average Salary:** $180,000 - $250,000
- **Range:** $150,000 - $400,000+ depending on company size and location

**Note:** Salaries vary significantly by geographic location, employer size, industry sector, and specific role. Salaries in major tech hubs (Silicon Valley, New York, Seattle) are typically 20-40% higher than national averages.
